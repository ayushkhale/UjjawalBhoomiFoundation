About Us

Who We Are

Ujjawal Bhoomi Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating sustainable, lasting change by working closely with underserved communities. We focus on education, healthcare, environmental conservation, and community empowerment to build a brighter tomorrow.

Our Mission

"To transform lives through sustainable community development programs that empower individuals, strengthen communities, and create opportunities for the most vulnerable members of our society."

Our Vision

A world where every individual has access to quality education, healthcare, clean environment, and the opportunity to live with dignity — building self-reliant communities from the ground up.

Our Values

Transparency, compassion, grassroots empowerment, and sustainable impact. We design programs that are community-driven, culturally sensitive, and built for lasting change across generations.

Our Journey

Building Impact
Since 2020

From a small group of passionate volunteers to a recognized community development organization — every milestone reflects our commitment to creating sustainable, meaningful change.

4+Years of Impact
1000+Lives Touched
Phase 01 2020

Foundation Established

Ujjawal Bhoomi Foundation was established with a vision to create lasting change in underserved communities across India.

  • Registered as a non-profit organization
  • Built the founding volunteer team
  • Identified key focus areas: education, health, environment
Phase 02 2021

Community Programs Launch

Launched our first community programs focusing on education, healthcare, and environmental awareness.

  • First plantation drive with local community
  • School supply distribution program
  • Community health awareness campaigns
Phase 03 2023

Strategic Partnerships

Expanded our reach through strategic partnerships with like-minded organizations and government bodies.

  • Partnership with Dakshi Foundation & Sangmitra Trust
  • Government-aligned environmental programs
  • Expanded to multiple districts in Madhya Pradesh
Phase 04 2025

Scaling Impact

Growing our programs, deepening our community roots, and building sustainable systems for long-term impact.

  • Digital presence and awareness campaigns
  • Advanced medical camp programs
  • Multi-state community engagement initiatives
