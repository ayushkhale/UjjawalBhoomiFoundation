Our Journey

Milestones of Impact

2020

Foundation Established

Ujjawal Bhoomi Foundation was registered as a non-profit with a founding team of passionate volunteers committed to grassroots change.

2021

First Community Programs

Launched plantation drives, school supply distributions, and community health awareness campaigns across Madhya Pradesh.

2023

Strategic Partnerships

Partnered with Dakshi Foundation, Sangmitra Trust, and others to expand reach into multiple districts and government-aligned programs.

2025

Scaling Impact

Expanding digital presence, advanced medical camps, and multi-state community engagement for deeper, broader impact.
